How to use it

From an empty matrix to a solver-backed decision in a few taps.

1 · Build a range

Tap cells in the 13×13 matrix, drop in a preset, or type standard notation — and save ranges to your library.

2 · Set the board

Pick the flop, turn and river cards you want to study.

3 · Read equity & breakdown

Instantly see how the range hits the board — made hands, draws, and equity versus another range.

4 · Study GTO

Open a solved spot and read per-hand action frequencies straight from the solver.

5 · Train

Quiz yourself hand after hand and get instant, solver-correct feedback.
