FlopVision

See the flop the way solvers do

What it is

  • Range editor with weighted 13×13 matrix and standard notation
  • How ranges hit boards: made-hand and draw breakdown for any flop, turn or river
  • Fast equity: hand vs range and range vs range, computed on your device
  • GTO strategy viewer: precomputed solver solutions with per-combo action frequencies

What it is not

  • No real-money gambling, no bets, no casino
  • No connection to poker sites — FlopVision is a study tool
  • No account, no tracking: everything runs offline on your device
FlopVision is an educational tool for studying poker theory. It does not offer gambling of any kind.
