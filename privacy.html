FlopVision — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Summary

FlopVision is an offline poker study tool. The app does not require an account, does not collect personal data, does not use advertising or analytics SDKs, and does not transmit your ranges, boards or calculations anywhere. All computation happens locally on your device.

Data we collect

None. FlopVision has no server of its own and sends no usage data to us. Your saved ranges and settings are stored only on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Subscriptions and purchases

If you purchase an optional Pro subscription, the payment is processed entirely by Google Play. We never see your name, payment details or address. To validate subscription status the app uses RevenueCat, a subscription-management service, which receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and purchase tokens from Google Play. This data cannot be used by us to identify you. RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy. Google Play's terms apply to all purchases.

Permissions

FlopVision requests no sensitive permissions: no location, no contacts, no camera, no storage access beyond its own sandbox.

Children

FlopVision is an educational strategy tool that contains poker themes and is not directed at children. It offers no real-money gambling.

Changes

If this policy changes (for example, if we ever add optional analytics), the updated version will be published at this address with a new effective date before the change ships.
