Frequently asked

Does the app need the internet?

No. All analysis runs offline on your device. Internet is only used to download optional extra strategy databases.

Is this real-money gambling?

No. FlopVision is a study tool. There is no betting, no casino, and no connection to poker sites.

How do subscriptions work?

Core features are free. An optional Pro subscription unlocks extra spots, deeper breakdowns and unlimited trainer hands. Manage or cancel it in your app-store account.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app and use "Restore purchases" on the Pro screen; purchases are tied to your store account.
